How to Recover Sbcglobal Email?

  • Go to the official AT&T sign-in page and click on “Forgot Password?”.
  • Enter your full @sbcglobal.net email address and last name.
  • Choose a recovery method (registered mobile number or recovery email) to receive a verification code.
  • Enter the verification code and follow the instructions to reset your password.
  • Create a new strong password and log in to your SBCGlobal email account.
